Welcome to Pup Parents!

Hi there! Welcome to Pup Parents, were our goal is to help you with your new addition to your household. If its your first time having a pup of your own or you just need a little help remembering what to do, we have the information you need.

Not only do we have training guides, but we also have recommended supplies you should get for your pup. We have a variety of recommendations of different brands, prices, and quality! The supplies we list are things like food, toys, accessories, health prodcuts. For food we catorgies them by breed size. Toys are listed for different stimulations. Accessories range from dog food bowl, collar, and more! Can't forget health products, we want your pup to be healthy too! So go check them out!

The reason I created this website because I too am a Pup Parent, I've had dogs in the past but never of my very own. Sometime around the beginning of August of 2023 I adopted my baby who is named Jack. He's the mascot for this website. Don't trust his cute face. Anyways I had to find a vet, I had to find the food he ate and see if that was good for him. I had to find training treats, toys, beds, etc. I also had to search up how to train him so he doesn't develop bad habits. Many of the sites I looked at ended up taking a while to find the one thing I needed. It was pain, I wished for a site only for puppies and had the things you need right up front. Thus! Pup Parents was born. I hope this website helps you find what you need. If you have any suggestions, please let us know! Our contact information can be found down below in a link. Have a nice day!
